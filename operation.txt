Non concealable guns must be put in the first slot in the hotbar to use
press zoom key to load guns (you must have the proper ammunition in your main inventory), drop key to unload.
while loading/unloading multiple rounds, you can use dig key (left mouse) to cancel loading.
while unloading you can press drop key again to drop the gun.
dig button (left mouse) to fire, place button (right mouse) to zoom in/out

magazines simply hold bullets, they are not consumed.
to load a magazine with bullets, put the magazine and the proper ammunition in the craft grid.
to unload the bullets from the magazine, put only the magazine in the craft grid.
you must unload the magazine with drop key before loading another with zoom key.
